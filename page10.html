QUALITY AND DELIVERY

At the core of NINE STARS are three principles that define us and the services that we provide: quality, integrity and delivery. These principles are deeply rooted in how we go to work. They are what has shaped our reputation and lead to our customers to come back, time after time. 

Our business relies upon satisfied customers who are willing to be our advocates, and we work tirelessly to develop relationships that are open, honest and backed by a responsive and flexible attitude. Our teams always take pride in what they do, delivering a very proactive approach to finding solutions and solving problems. 

QUALITY 

We have a clear understanding of the processes that ensure effective quality management and project delivery, and we recognize that they are only valid if we put into place and maintain appropriate resources, components and a disciplined approach.

Experienced construction teams, leading our projects, ensure that we deliver a 'right first time', quality product that exceeds our Clients' expectations.

We select from the industries' best, prequalify and ensure effective alignment of our supply chain with our vision, values and expectations. With a partnership approach to delivery, our key trade companies competently perform and produce the required standards time and again. Our best in class supply chain management system measures, tracks and records performance information across our business and ensure our preferred supply chain list remains effective.

We strive to make every project as exceptional in its appearance, finish and functionality.


DELIVERY

At NINE STARS we have a proven track record of consistently delivering complex projects on time, and to budget. Our project teams have the knowledge, skills, capability and capacity to realize Clients' expectations and this is demonstrated by the NINE STARS portfolio of completed projects across the UAE.

We believe in working collaboratively with our Clients, consultants and supply chain to ensure delivery in line with agreed programs. Team-working and a collaborative approach are always key to successful project delivery.
