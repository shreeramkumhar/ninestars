SKILLS & EXPERTISE



When companies work with Nine Stars, they expect high quality construction services, business integrity and on time delivery. But we strive to provide much more.Nine Stars has the capability to provide a wide range of complementary skills and expertise ranging from pre construction through project delivery to post completion and aftercare services.

PRECONSTRUCTION

Our preferred way of working is in a collaborative manner with all stakeholders to reach the most efficiently delivered project in terms of cost, time, safety and quality.

By providing a preconstruction service to Customers we can achieve robust cost and buildable construction solutions, make an earlier start on site compared to traditional, and overall achieve higher levels of project outcome certainty

PROJECT DELIVERY

Nine Stars reputation for quality and integrity is derived from over 14 years of experience in delivering construction projects in the UAE.

We have trusted project processes and systems that are proven to work and have provided our customers with on time delivery and high.quality products time after time.


POST COMPLETION

Post completion is an area of great focus for Nine Stars because we realize how important it is for our customers to get a building completed and in use quickly and efficiently as possible.

PRECONSTRUCTION

FEASIBILITY

During the feasibility process, we can assist with high level cost estimates, outline programming and initial build-ability assessments so your feasibility can include real life construction knowledge and data

BUILD ABILITY

During the design process, we can provide advice on the most suitable solutions, what works best and what not work, what will be quick and efficient, and what may be problematic when on site, thus equipping clients with a practical and buildable design.

PLANNING AND LOGISTICS

We can provide innovative planning solutions to ensure projects are efficiently planned, properly sequenced and the logistics are methodically considered with real time solutions and fast paced construction techniques.

BUDGETING AND COST PLANNING

Our estimation team can provide customers with a range of cost planning services as part of their feasibility and build-ability studies. We ensure our customers are presented with realistic project budgets and cost plans to avoid unwelcome shocks or surprises at a later time

VALUE ENGINEERING

Our pre‑construction teams can assess a design and identify areas where cost and / or time savings could be realized without reducing the overall function of a building or the value that users would experience.

FURNITURE FIXTURES AND EQUIPMENT (FF&E)  

Our dedicated FF&E team provides design and procurement services as early as possible to ensure value for money and on time procurement.

BUILDING INFORMATION MODELING (BIM)

BIM is a powerful tool for managing information on a construction project. We drive the use of BIM to reduce construction costs by reducing rework and design changes, eliminating service clashes, and improving coordination.

PROJECT MANAGEMENT


Our project management teams coordinate all aspects of complex construction projects between suppliers and subcontractors to consultants and the authorities. In short, they are responsible for delivering high quality projects to our clients on time and on budget.

DESIGN MANAGEMENT

Our team of Design Managers coordinate all elements of design principles and aspects; structural design to shop drawings, provide design detailing services and assist with the practical resolution of design issues. If BIMs are used, they run clash detection systems, produce walkthroughs and simulate congested areas to eliminate costly & time‑consuming design problems.

PROGRAM MANAGEMENT 

During the construction process, our planning teams are based in our sites to provide the team and clients with up to date progress and planning information. They facilitate on‑time delivery and coordination of design information, materials, subcontractors and equipment to achieve milestone dates and on budget delivery.

SUPPLY CHAIN MANAGEMENT

We only work with the best suppliers and subcontractors available which enhances our ability to deliver projects successfully to our customers. All our major subcontractors and suppliers undergo an extensive pre‑qualification process to ensure their capabilities match those required by the project, and that they have the management systems and resources to deliver when needed. 

SUBCONTRACTOR & MATERIAL PROCUREMENT

We ensure that our clients receive the best level of service at competitive market prices by procuring the best suppliers and subcontractors that provide the most cost‑effective solutions for our clients.

TENANT LIAISON

PROJECT CLOSE TO

We provide high quality Facilities Management services that provides our clients with the confidence that their building is being managed and maintained by the same company that built it.
